What is TSL?

Trailing StopLoss moves UP with price but never moves down. Locks in profit automatically.

🚀
When to Start Trailing

Start trailing only after price rises by ₹10+ from your entry. Before that, keep fixed SL.

📱
Groww has no Auto-TSL

Do it manually. Every ₹5 rise → move SL up by ₹5. Check every 5–10 minutes.

🔒
Zero-Loss Setup

Once price rises ₹10 from entry, move SL to your buy price. Now you can't lose!

⚡
Trail Gap Rule

Keep TSL gap = your original SL amount. If SL was ₹7, trail ₹7 below current price always.
